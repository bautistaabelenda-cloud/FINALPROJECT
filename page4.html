      who is it?

      Merab Dvalishvili is a Georgian mixed martial artist who competes in the UFC’s bantamweight division. He is known for his incredible cardio, relentless pressure, and unstoppable wrestling style. Merab often breaks his opponents with constant takedown attempts and high pace, earning him the nickname “The Machine.” He trains with top fighters like Aljamain Sterling in the United States and has built a reputation as one of the toughest and most hardworking athletes in the division. Many fans see him as a future title contender because of his strong work ethic and dominant performances.
