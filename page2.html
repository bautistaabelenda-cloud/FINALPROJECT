About UFC

The UFC (Ultimate Fighting Championship) is a mixed martial arts (MMA) organization that brings together the world's best fighters across various weight classes. In their fights, athletes can use various disciplines such as boxing, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, kickboxing, wrestling, and muay thai, among others. The UFC was founded in 1993 in the United States and has over time become the most important and recognized league in the world in this sport. Its events are broadcast internationally and combine spectacle, strategy, and high physical performance.
